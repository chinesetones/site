The fun way to master Chinese tones.


  

Why use Ka?

Sharpen your ear.

The most difficult thing in Chinese is tones. Learn to recognize the 4 tones, and remember when to use which.

Shi, Si or Xi?

Train your ear to pick up nuances in pronunciation. Be able to tell the difference between similar syllables and sounds.

Fun and addictive.

Quick, snappy drills that make learning a breeze. Earn stars and level up while getting repeated Chinese practice!

Speak naturally.

Speaking exercises that tell you if you mispronounce any word or tone. Work towards a more natural Chinese accent!

No more mistakes.

No more confusing mā (mother) for mǎ (horse), or shāng hài (hurt) for shàng hǎi (Shanghai)!
