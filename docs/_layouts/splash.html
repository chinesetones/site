Why use Ka?


Sharpen your ear.

Train your ear to pick up nuances in pronunciation. Be able to tell the difference between similar syllables and sounds.

Quick, snappy drills that make learning a breeze. Earn stars and level up while getting repeated Chinese tones practice!

Research shows that being better able to hear sounds will improve your pronunication ability. Work towards a more natural Chinese accent!
